Rules

The rules are simple

To gain points, you must acquire as much badges as possible.

For every badge you receive you gain a certain amount of points(refer to above) which increases your Rank within the Leaderboard.

*Note. You automatically receive 10 Points for attending three(3) consecutive meetings on time.
